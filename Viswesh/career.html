About Us

Who Are We?
MedPlus Pharmacy is India’s second largest retail pharmacy chain with its presence in 7 states, 250+ cities with a vast network of 2200+ brick and mortar stores spread across the country. We also host our online shopping platforms www.medplusmart.com and MedPlusMart mobile app where you can find not only prescription medicines and OTC medicines but also general essentials for your house and daily lives. Our legacy of 15 years has been built on the foundation of providing genuine and authentic medicines at the highest discount making medicines easily affordable and accessible to anybody at any given point in time!

We Are Leading For a Reason:
Our FLAT 20% discount on medicines purchase on every single order makes us the leader in best value for the customer’s money. With about 15,000+ employees, we serve and satisfy about 13 million customers every month.

Largest Omnichannel Pharmacy Platform:
Primarily, our customers trust us because we provide high quality genuine and safe medicines at the highest discount in India; and also great discounts on the general products throughout the year. Additionally, our guaranteed next day home delivery allows our customers to conveniently place their order from their homes and rest assured, receive their medicines in one day or less! Customers can either walk-in to our air conditioned stores or order online for home delivery/ store pickup. This makes us an omnichannel platform allowing customers to suit their preferences.


Overall, we are driven by providing the best value for our customers’ money
